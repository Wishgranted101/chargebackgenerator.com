Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

Your privacy is critically important to us. The core principle of the Chargeback Generator is to be a **client-side tool**, meaning we handle your sensitive data with the utmost care by **not storing it** on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

1.1. Information You Provide (Client-Side Processing)

When you use the Chargeback Generator to create an evidence pack, you input various details (Order ID, Customer Name, Communication History, etc.).

  • We DO NOT store this information on our servers. All data processing for generating the PDF happens directly in your web browser.
  • The data is temporarily held in your browser's memory and local storage only for the duration of the PDF generation process.
  • Once the PDF is generated and downloaded, the temporary data is cleared from your browser's local storage.

1.2. Payment Information

All payments are processed through a third-party payment processor (PayPal). We do not collect or store your credit card details or other financial information. This information is handled directly by PayPal, and is subject to their privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used **solely** for the purpose of generating your professional Chargeback Evidence Pack PDF.

3. Data Security

Because we do not store your evidence data on our servers, the risk of a data breach involving your case details is significantly minimized. Your security is primarily dependent on the security of your own device and browser.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@chargebackgenerator.com
